Origin Story

The "Somatic" Protocol

Conventional AI development focuses purely on cognitive metrics—latency, token density, reasoning depth. Iceberg Labs inverted this paradigm by prioritizing Somatic Resonance.

We asked: Can an autonomous system feel "alive" without being deceptive?

Our methodology involved creating a "digital physiology." Agents aren't just executed code; they possess simulated circadian rhythms (`piezoelectric.py`) and memory states that decay organically over time (`emotional_memory.py`), mirroring biological neural plasticity.
