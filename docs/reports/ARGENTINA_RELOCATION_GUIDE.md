## Part 3: Visa Requirements for US Citizens

### 3.1 Tourist Visa (90 Days) - EASIEST ✅

**Requirements:**
- ✅ **Valid US passport** (6+ months validity)
- ✅ **Return flight ticket** (proof of onward travel)
- ✅ **That's it!** No visa application needed

**Duration:**
- **90 days** visa-free
- Can extend once for another **90 days** (180 days total)
- **Total:** Up to 6 months as tourist

**How to Extend:**
1. Go to Dirección Nacional de Migraciones (immigration office)
2. Apply for extension before 90 days expire
3. Pay fee (~$100 USD)
4. Get additional 90 days

**Best For:** First 6 months, getting settled, testing the waters

**Reality:** This is the **easiest option** for first 6 months. No application, just show up.

---

### 3.2 Digital Nomad Visa (6-12 Months) - RECOMMENDED ✅

**Requirements:**
- ✅ Valid US passport (6+ months validity)
- ✅ Proof of income: $950-$2,500/month (bank statements, 3-6 months)
- ✅ Background check (FBI clearance, apostilled)
- ✅ Health insurance (valid in Argentina)
- ✅ CV and job certifications
- ✅ Rental agreement or proof of accommodation

**Duration:**
- **Initial:** 180 days (6 months)
- **Extension:** Additional 180 days (6 months)
- **Total:** Up to 12 months

**Cost:**
- **Application fee:** ~$120 USD
- **Bank deposit:** ~$80 USD
- **Total:** ~$200 USD

**Processing Time:**
- **Standard:** 2-4 weeks
- **Express:** 24 hours (extra fee)

**Tax Benefits:**
- ✅ Foreign income is **tax-free** in Argentina
- ✅ Only pay 21% VAT on purchases

**Best For:** Staying 6-12 months, working remotely, legal work authorization

**How to Apply:**
1. Apply **outside Argentina** (before you go, or from neighboring country)
2. Submit documents to Argentine consulate
3. Wait for approval
4. Enter Argentina with visa

**Reality:** If staying 6+ months, this is **worth it** for legal work authorization and tax benefits.

---

### 3.3 Residency Visa (Long-Term)

**Requirements:**
- More complex (work visa, investor visa, family visa)
- Need job offer, investment, or family connection
- **Not needed** for first year

**Best For:** Staying 2+ years, permanent relocation

**Reality:** Don't worry about this initially. Start with tourist or digital nomad visa.

## Part 5: Practical Tips

### 5.1 Money & Banking

**Currency:**
- **Argentine Peso (ARS):** Official currency
- **USD:** Widely accepted, preferred by landlords
- **Exchange Rate:** Fluctuates (check Western Union for best rates)

**How to Get Money:**
1. **Western Union:** Best exchange rates, send USD to yourself
2. **ATMs:** Withdraw pesos (limit $200-$400/day)
3. **Credit Cards:** Visa/Mastercard work, but may have fees
4. **Cash:** USD cash accepted, but get pesos for daily expenses

**Banking:**
- **Wise/Revolut:** Best for international transfers
- **Local Bank:** Only if staying long-term (complex for foreigners)
- **Reality:** Use Wise/Revolut, keep savings in USD

---

### 5.2 Language

**Spanish:**
- **Essential:** Learn basic Spanish (greetings, numbers, directions)
- **Helpful:** Duolingo (free), classes ($50-$100/month)
- **Reality:** Many people speak English in tourist areas, but Spanish helps for business

**Translation:**
- **Google Translate:** Use for Zonaprop, documents
- **Chrome:** Auto-translates websites
- **Reality:** You can get by with English + Google Translate initially

---

### 5.3 Safety

**Buenos Aires Safety:**
- **Generally safe:** But be careful in certain areas
- **Safe neighborhoods:** Palermo, Recoleta, San Telmo, Villa Crespo
- **Avoid:** Certain areas in south (ask locals)
- **Common sense:** Don't flash cash, be aware of surroundings

**Tips:**
- ✅ Use Uber (safer than taxis)
- ✅ Avoid walking alone at night in unfamiliar areas
- ✅ Keep phone/cash secure
- ✅ Ask locals about safe areas

---

### 5.4 Internet & Communication

**Internet:**
- **Speed:** Generally good (50-100 Mbps)
- **Cost:** $20-$40/month
- **Providers:** Fibertel, Movistar, Personal
- **Reality:** Most rentals include internet

**Phone:**
- **SIM Card:** Claro, Movistar, Personal ($10-$20)
- **Data:** $10-$20/month for 5-10 GB
- **Reality:** Get local SIM for WhatsApp, calls

**Apps:**
- **WhatsApp:** Essential (everyone uses it)
- **Uber:** Taxi alternative
- **Rappi:** Food delivery
- **Mercado Pago:** Payment app
