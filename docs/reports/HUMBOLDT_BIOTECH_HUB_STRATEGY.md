**Prepared for:** Cal Poly Humboldt & Humboldt County Economic Development

**Focus:** Leveraging ICEBURG for Community Health Research & Economic Revitalization

## Executive Summary

Cal Poly Humboldt has strong health/medical science programs but **lacks biotech industry presence**. ICEBURG can bridge this gap by enabling **affordable, non-invasive community health studies** that:
1. **Attract research funding** (NSF, NIH, private foundations)
2. **Bring biotech companies** to the region
3. **Revitalize local economy** (Arcata/Eureka)
4. **Position Humboldt as innovation hub** (not just environmental)

**Key Opportunity:** ICEBURG enables **$0-equipment studies** using consumer hardware, making Humboldt County competitive for research funding despite limited infrastructure.
